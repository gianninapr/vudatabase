The Astronomy and Astrophysics Department at Villanova University has been in ownership of
an extensive catalog of variables and eclipsing binaries for a few years now. This year, Dr.
Andrej Prsa decided to make the digitizing of the archive a project for students Giannina
Guzman, and Briana Dalton.

This project includes:

  1. The creation of an empty database
  2. The data-entry
  3. The processing of said data
  4. The production of a website showing said data

Up to now, the team has worked with Django to create an empty database and an interface for
data-entry. The next step will require volunteer help to enter the data into the database.
Since this archive is extensive, and handwritten, this process will take some time, and
it might prove to be one of the most challenging parts of the project.

Once the data is entered in the database, the team will work on publicizing the data online
by developing a website that will carry and display the data entered in the database. This
website will be made to showcase the archival data that had been buried for years in metal
containers somewhere in a cold room.
